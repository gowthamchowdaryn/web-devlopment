Meesho Cart
Meesho is an online shopping platform offering a wide variety of affordable products, from clothing and home essentials to electronics and more. The cart system on Meesho provides a smooth shopping experience, allowing users to review and manage their orders before finalizing their purchase.
Below are some key features of the Meesho Store and its cart system:

1. Product Categories

Fashion: Shop for trendy clothing, shoes, accessories, and more for women, men, and kids.
Home Essentials: Buy kitchenware, furniture, decor items, and household products.
Electronics: Purchase mobile accessories, small appliances, smart gadgets, and other tech items.
Beauty & Personal Care: Browse skincare, haircare, makeup products, and fragrances.
Toys & Baby Care: Shop for toys, baby clothes, and baby care essentials.

2. Cart Features

Add to Cart: Easily add products to your cart for later review before purchase.
View and Edit Cart: Check item details, adjust quantities, or remove items from the cart.
Price Breakdown: View itemized costs, including discounts, taxes, and applicable offers.
Wishlist: Save items to your wishlist for later purchase.

3. Checkout Process

Secure Payment Options: Pay with various methods such as credit cards, UPI, debit cards, or Meesho wallet.
Shipping and Delivery: Select preferred delivery options for different product types.
Order Confirmation: Receive order confirmation and shipping details via email or SMS.

4. Benefits of Shopping on Meesho

Exclusive Deals: Enjoy discounts, cashback offers, and special promotions on selected products.
Reseller Benefits: Earn commissions by reselling products to your customers via the app.
24/7 Customer Support: Get assistance with your order, returns, or any other inquiries.
Easy Returns: Meesho provides an easy return and refund process for eligible products.

5. Personalized Recommendations

Meesho suggests products based on your browsing and purchase history.
It recommends items based on trends, seasonal offers, and complementary items.

6. Compatibility Checks

Meesho ensures that products meet quality standards, with detailed descriptions to confirm compatibility with customer preferences.
